About

Tasfia Mahmud is a visual artist whose work navigates the quiet spaces between memory and sensation. Drawing from the interplay of natural light, organic textures, and the subtle rhythms of everyday life, her pieces invite viewers to pause and feel.

Working primarily with mixed media, watercolor, and ink, Tasfia builds layered compositions that balance delicacy with intention. Each piece begins as an intuitive response — a color, a gesture, a fragment of thought — and evolves through a meditative process of addition and restraint.

Her work has been exhibited in group and solo shows, and lives in private collections. She is currently based in Dhaka, creating from her home studio.
